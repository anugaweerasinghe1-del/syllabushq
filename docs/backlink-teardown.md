## Signal vs noise

The competitors' top referring domains are heavily contaminated with Telegram
PBN spam (`@seo_cartel`, `@seo_anomaly`, `tg @bhs_links`) — do NOT emulate
that. Filter to real editorial/community links only:

### savemyexams.com — the ones worth chasing
- **yahoo.com** (AS 100) — press mentions / roundup coverage
- **bbc.co.uk** (AS 100) — cited as a study resource in BBC Bitesize–adjacent articles
- **slyacademy.com / revisiontown.com / kumaticseducation.com** — teacher & tutor blogs
- Anchor `mark scheme` — high-intent, page-title-driven

### tutopiya.com
- **edtechagency.net** (AS 23) — edtech directory listing
- **laotiantimes.com** (AS 39) — regional press coverage

### pastpapers.wiki
- **agaram.lk** (AS 12) — Sri Lankan Tamil-medium student portal
- Long tail of `.lk` teacher blogs and forum embeds

## Target referring-domain types (prioritised for LK)

1. **`.ac.lk` / school resource pages** — Royal, Ananda, Visakha, D.S. Senanayake, Musaeus, etc. Cold email teachers with a printable question pack.
2. **agaram.lk, e-learning.lk, pastpapers.lk** — LK-specific student portals; ask for a resource-page listing.
3. **Reddit r/srilanka + r/Sri_Lanka** — value-first post, not a link-drop.
4. **YouTube tutor channels (SL)** — Sirasa TV lessons, Guru Gedara, Hiru Educational — offer the daily-question embed for their community tab.
5. **Edtech directories** — Product Hunt, BetaList, edtechagency.net, Alternativeto.
6. **Sri Lankan parenting/education blogs** — Ceylon Today education column, Roar Media, Sunday Times education page.
7. **GitHub awesome-lists** — `awesome-sri-lanka`, `awesome-education`.
