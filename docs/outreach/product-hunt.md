# Product Hunt launch — SyllabusHQ

## Tagline (60 char max)

> Free, AI-marked O/L exam practice for Sri Lankan students.

## Description (260 char max)

> Original MCQs, real NIE-format structured papers, and short-answer drills for Sri Lanka's G.C.E. Ordinary Level. An AI chief examiner grades your written answers point-by-point against a real marking scheme. No login. Free forever.

## First comment (from maker)

> Hey Product Hunt 👋
>
> I built SyllabusHQ because every "past-paper" site I found for Sri Lankan
> O/Ls was either a PDF dump, riddled with ads, or a paywalled coaching
> upsell. Meanwhile the students who need it most — kids in Colombo suburbs
> and rural areas without tuition access — get nothing.
>
> Three things make this different:
>
> 1. **AI marking.** You type an answer, an AI trained on real O/L marking
>    schemes grades it point-by-point — not just "yes/no". That's the
>    feedback loop that actually moves grades.
> 2. **Real paper structures.** Every mode mirrors the exact NIE format
>    (Part A × 25 × 2 marks, Part B answer 5 of 10, etc.) — not a generic
>    quiz builder.
> 3. **Free forever.** No login, no signup wall, no ads. Ever.
>
> Would love your feedback — especially from anyone who's built for
> emerging-market education. What did I miss?
